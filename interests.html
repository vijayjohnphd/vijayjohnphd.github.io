Research Fields & Interests

🧠

Multimodal Deep Learning

Sensor fusion, missing-modality robustness, cross-modal learning, modality imputation, and multimodal portability.

🚘

Autonomous Driving

Perception, path planning, lane estimation, obstacle detection, and vision-based vehicle control.

🤖

Robot Perception

Humanoid robot vision, human-robot interaction, gesture & sign language recognition, and social robot development.

📷

Computer Vision

Semantic segmentation, object detection, stereo vision, 3D reconstruction, and spatio-temporal learning.

📡

Sensor Fusion

Camera–LIDAR–RADAR–thermal fusion; deep Bayesian inference; automatic sensor calibration without targets.

📊

Representation Learning

Metric learning, embedding learning, distance metric optimisation, self-supervised and transfer learning.

🔊

Audio-Visual Learning

Multimodal transformers for emotion recognition, sound event detection, and co-speech gesture generation.

🌐

Weak & Semi-Supervised

Training action recognition and detection models with sequence-level weak labels to reduce annotation cost.
